Welcome to this Executive Villa with Private Pool, an exceptional residence located in one of Islamabad's most prestigious neighborhoods, F-7/2. Built in 2019 to the highest standards of luxury living, the home spans 500 square yards and offers five spacious bedrooms, four custom bathrooms, and a thoughtfully designed open-plan living area that flows seamlessly into the outdoor pool deck.

The architecture combines contemporary lines with timeless materials — imported Italian marble, full-height windows, and bespoke joinery throughout. Every room is finished with double-glazed windows, central air conditioning, and a smart home automation system controlling lighting, climate, and security from a single interface.

Set behind a double-boundary wall with 24/7 CCTV coverage and a 25 kVA backup generator, the property delivers privacy, security, and ease of living rarely found at this scale. With dedicated servant quarters, four covered parking bays, and easy access to F-7 Markaz, Islamabad Club, and Margalla Road, this is an opportunity for buyers seeking a turnkey luxury residence in the capital's most desirable address.
